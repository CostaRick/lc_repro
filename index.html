What sets us apart

Teaching method

We teach according to the European Framework of Reference for Languages. With our motivating teaching material we train speaking, reading, listening and writing in an entertaining but structured way.

Our teachers

We are German state-trained teachers with several years of professional experience in schools and in online teaching.

Fast results

Our methods are based on the latest neuroscientific foundations so that you can learn to speak fluently quickly.

Adaptive to the student's needs

Everyone has different reasons and prior knowledge. We adapt to your needs and develop a learning strategy that suits you.

How flexible are the lessons?

Each lesson is a private lesson with you. You can choose between lessons in Skype or Zoom.
Our booking calendar can be found here. Book an appointment there and we will call you in Skype or Zoom.
You can cancel or rebook any private lesson free of charge up to 6 hours before the lesson.

Isn't German too difficult for me?

German is a very structured language, so that also some structures have to be learned. We have very good learning material, which is provided for you free of charge. Our lessons are fun and varied. You determine the pace, we make sure that the lessons are easy for you.

How do I pay?

We use Paypal or Stripe as payment methods. These online variants are easy to understand. So you can book your lessons without much effort and previous knowledge. If you buy an lesson package we will inform you before the hours are used up.
